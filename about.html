About Me


I like to innovate, empower, provide opportunities, and create impact

Hi, I'm Edna Sawe - a scientist, a developer and a passionate entreprenuer. I like to create things that have an impact in people's lives.

I always aim to write clean and elegant code everyday. Thanks to Udacity, Women Techmakers, and Google for making this possible. Now, my goal is to give back and impact more lives!

With my passion in technology and energy, I will be taking you through my journey as an entreprenuer. I will be sharing lessons learnt and trending news that could be of great benefit to us all as we work on building a better future.

I will also be posting on events and opportunities in technology and entreprenuership for students and passionate entreprenuers. For those interested and would like to learn more about a specific career path, there will be VR tours of various professions that would help answer all the questions that you need to know about the professions.

There are also opportunities and programs for kids between the ages of 10 and 18 to learn game development, robotics, and coding using HTML/CSS.
